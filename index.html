Hi there!

I'm a Full Stack Web Developer living in Houston, TX. I was born and raised in Houston, and have spent my entire life living in Houston and a suburb named Sugar Land. I attended the University of Houston for my undergraduate degree where I got a BBA majoring in Finance. I have always been interested in Computer Science. When I was younger, I was tempted to major in it, but I decided instead to go the Finance route. I quickly realized that my passion was elsewhere upon graduating.

I spent one year teaching myself coding through online tutorials (and a lot of trial and error!) by building small apps and websites. I realized I loved programming and wanted to pursue it as a career. I am fascinated by the endless possibilities of things you can do and create if you have the creative mind for it. I graduated from DigitalCrafts' four month, full-time immersive coding bootcamp January of 2018.

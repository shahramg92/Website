Hi there!

I'm a Full Stack Web Developer living in Austin, TX. I was born and raised in Houston, and have spent my entire life living in Houston and a suburb named Sugar Land. I attended the University of Houston for my undergraduate degree where I got a BBA majoring in Finance. I've always been interested in Computer Science when I was younger and was tempted to major in it, but decided to go the Finance route. I quickly realized that my passion was elsewhere upon graduating.

I spent 6 months teaching myself coding through online tutorials (and a lot of trial and error!) and building websites and for myself and friends. I realized I loved programming and wanted to pursue it as a career. I am fascinated by the endless possibilities of things you can do and create if you have the creative mind for it. I am graduating from DigitalCrafts four month, full-time immersive coding bootcamp in January of 2018.
